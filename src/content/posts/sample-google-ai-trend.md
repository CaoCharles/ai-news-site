# Google Unveils Gemini 2.0

Google has officially announced the release of **Gemini 2.0**, marking a significant leap forward in multimodal AI capabilities.

## Key Features

*   **Enhanced Reasoning**: Solves complex math and coding problems with higher accuracy.
*   **Multimodal Native**: Built from the ground up to understand text, images, video, and audio simultaneously.
*   **Efficiency**: Runs faster and more efficiently on TPU v5p chips.

> "This is the beginning of a new era for AI," said Sundar Pichai.

The model will be available to developers starting next week via Google Cloud Vertex AI.
